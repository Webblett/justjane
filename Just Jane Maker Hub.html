🔧 Troubleshooting

Ghosting

Secure paper tightly with heat tape; do not move blank immediately after pressing.

Colour too light

Increase time by 10 sec; check ink cartridge install date and expiry.

White spots / moisture marks

Pre-press blank for 5 sec before sublimating to remove moisture.

Bleeding / smearing

Reduce pressure slightly; ensure paper is fully dry before pressing.

📦 Filament Storage Rules

  • Target humidity: <40% RH
  • Target temp: 18–22°C
  • Store in sealed containers with silica gel desiccant
  • Replace desiccant when indicator shows >40%
  • Shelf life — PLA: 1–2 yrs | PETG: 2+ yrs | TPU: 1 yr
  • Wet filament signs: popping, rough surface, bubbles

🧪 Resin Quick-Reference

  • UV Wavelength: 405nm
  • Standard resin: 6 bottom layers, 45s bottom, 3.5s normal
  • ABS-like resin: 6 bottom layers, 50s bottom, 4.0s normal

SAFETY

Always wear nitrile gloves, work in ventilated area, UV-cure all waste before disposal.
